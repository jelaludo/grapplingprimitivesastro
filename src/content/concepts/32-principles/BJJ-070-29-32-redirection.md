Sounds like Lu (履) in Taichi – Rolling/Deflecting Force (Yield & Redirect)
In biomechanics it would be rotational pivot around a central axis (like shoulder/hip/forearm), using circular redirection.

Application is to evades force by leading it off-center, like a door swinging, "using their force against them".
