"a pound of prevention is worth a ton of cure".  -- Benjamin Franklin, 1735, probably.  He was not talking about BJJ, or medicine, but fire safety of Philadelphia.  Same concept.  Deal with problems before they get worse.  Deny that pesky pant grip, keep your elbows tight before someone puts on a Dagestani cuff on.
