Establishing a connection with the opponent is how we ensure that our movements affects their center of gravity and balance and ability to recover.

My take on it is that you can;
grab someone and have no connection.
grab some cloth (gi) and have some connection if there's cloth-tension.

having an keeping connection requires some level of training and proprioception awareness.

it is most obviously achieved through the bones, either very obvious connection like hip-bone to hip-bone in a mount escape scenario, or more subtle, like grabbing a wrist and creating enough tension that it allows you to manipulate their shoulder or more.
