Act fast once you have a dominant grip.

"Grips Don't Getter With Time" is how I first remember hearing it from Marcelo Garcia.

No Grip is Eternal.

Opponents start to fight against them, probe the weaknesses, change angles forcing re-gripping.

Don't miss the window of opportunity.
