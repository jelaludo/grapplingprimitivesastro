Create positions where all choices are undesirable for the opponent.

(etymology, greek, "double proposition")

Mount : Cross-collar Choke <> Armbar
Back : RNC / Armbar / Rear Triangle, etc.
Guard : Triangle/Armbar/Omoplata
Side : Mouse trap (Americana/Straight/Kimura)

Collar Tie + Wrist Control : disengage >> shoot, fight >> snapdown

Pressure : Stay and Suffer, or Escape and Expose

Uchimata > ankle pick/snap down/ko uchi


Chaining Attacks is the mechanism
Dilemma is the effect, they know you can chain something.

Maybe chaining is the engine that produces dilemmas.

you can chain and try to overwhelm their ability to solve everything, or chain in a way that brings a Check Mate situation.

Chaining attacks is perhaps more about keeping momentum

Dilemma is a more of a controlled impossible choice.
