They're the fat pads at the base of the thumb and the base of the little finger.  Crucial to control the hand.

use the opponent's Thenar and Hypothenar eminences as control point and levers for wristlocks, use your own for grips and chin-straps.

An awareness of those muscles allows more precise control in grips, with mechanical advantage and reduction in tension to fatigue more slowly.
