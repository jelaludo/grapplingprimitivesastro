The Shin-Bone.

For knee-slices, knee-on-belly, leg-ride, shin-on-shin.

The ridge of the shin acts as a slicing or grinding tool When driven across the opponent’s body, it is sharp and creates discomfort and disrupts alignment.

Also key to angle it properly for calf-slicers.
