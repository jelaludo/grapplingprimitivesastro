The latissimus dorsi forms a powerful link between your arms and core. I mainly focus on that when doing armpit grips a la Silver Fox.

Whether you're dragging, collar-tying, or maintaining a clamp, lats stabilize the shoulder girdle and transmit force efficiently. Lat connection ensures pulling doesn’t rely only on biceps or grip, but the full body chain.
