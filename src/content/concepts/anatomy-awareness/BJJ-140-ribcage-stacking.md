Stacking the ribs over the pelvis to prevent rib flare, maintain neutral spine alignment, and enhance intra-abdominal pressure for stability

This creates a "stacked" tower from ankles through pelvis, ribcage, and head, supporting exercises like planks or roll-downs. Instructors cue it during breathing drills to expand the ribcage evenly without collapsing forward
