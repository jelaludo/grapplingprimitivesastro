#2/21 by Paulo Guillobel.

The Chess Analogy is certainly not *Uniquely* appropriate to grappling.  Football, soccer, or any sports involving positioning and decisions will have some chess analogies.   Pieces have values, controlling the center is advantageous, be 3 moves ahead, etc.
