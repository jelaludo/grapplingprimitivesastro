e.g. a coach had a phase during which he rolled without grabbing anything, you can force it by holding a tennis ball.

Never submit a white belt twice with the same submission.

Always start in a bad position.

Use only your non-dominant side.

Pass Guard without the hands

Eyes closed (makes some partners uncomfortable, discuss before hand)

Gauntlet, fresh opponents every round.
