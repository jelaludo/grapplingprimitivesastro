Coach sets the scenarios, but doesn't micromanage.

That's the essence of the ecological approach.  Athletes learn by solving problems in realistic, variable game-like situations, guided by constraints instead of explicit step-by-step instruction.

Rediscovering a principle by yourself makes it easier to ingrain, as opposed to being spoon-fed it.

self-discovery under realistic constraints builds deeper retention and adaptability than receiving the answer, but maybe not being confident that you can implement it.
