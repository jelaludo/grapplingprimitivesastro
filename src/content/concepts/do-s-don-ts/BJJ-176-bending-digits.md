Bending individual fingers is a big no-no in training. 

If you can twist and break a partner's fingers, surely they can retaliate by twisting and breaking one of yours.  Finger for Finger and the whole world has broken hands.

you can grab the palm.
grabbing four fingers is ok to break a grip.
the IBJJF rule is "three or more fingers" is generally ok to grab.
