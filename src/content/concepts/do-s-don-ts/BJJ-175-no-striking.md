Punches, kicks, elbows, knees, headbutts.  Grappling training is neither MMA nor Lethwei nor a vale tudo street fight.

It's important to set-aside some time for at least the awareness that someone might use these tools, and therefoe have a sense of control to prevent them.  But it's not something we can train full on without getting hurt.
