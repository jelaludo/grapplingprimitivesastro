Uncontrolled Weight
Throws become dangerous when uncontrolled weight can fall onto the opponent’s leg.

Tani-Otoshi from Judo and Kani-Basami Scissor throw are two frequently used examples.  The result is similar to the nasty Jon Jones knee kick, it forces a dramatic inside rotation beyond the safe range of motion and can easily rip tendons and meniscus apart.

Jumping guard
Jumping armbar
Jumping triangle
Uncontrolled heel-hook from top by throwing the weight from a leg-drag position.

Throwing one's weight at an angle on a smaller lighter training partner.

just be mindful not to hurt your partners, it takes effort, make it a habit.
