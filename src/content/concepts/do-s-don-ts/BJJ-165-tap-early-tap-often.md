The interesting part of learning happened before you got stuck that deep into a submission that is not threatening your with injuries.

There absolutely *exists a place for training late escapes, but that is absolutely not as a beginner, and not during every roll, and only with training partners you trust and who've mastered the art to apply submissions in a fully controlled manner.
