They tap, you stop.  No : "it wasn't fully on!", "come on that twister wasn't even finished!", etc.

If they tap you let go.  We're not inside our opponent's head or bodies, we don't fully know their injuries.

respect the tap, always.

Verbal, hand, double-tap, foot, all of the above. respect the tap.
