There's an element of puzzle-solving in BJJ that scratches a cognitive itch.  Every opponent is slightly different, either in build, experience or intensity.  Each with a  unique set of reactions, habits, and structural patterns. Training becomes a lively back-and-forth, your strategies are built, tested, broken, and refined. It's the joy of figuring things out, over and over again.
