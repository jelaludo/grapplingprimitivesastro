Some people, perhaps especially older black belts, perhaps train simply because they're curious. What happens if I try this grip? Can I solve that escape? Grappling becomes a sandbox for experimentation,an endless field of biomechanical and strategic possibilities, you constantly think of your body in three dimensions and how it entertwines with that of the opponent.

Not every skill or move we learn has to be the absolute best and most effective meta.
