Similar to Pivot.

Any rotation of the body around a fixed point (foot, knee, hip, hand, elbow) to escape of reorient your angle of attack without giving up base.

The pivot changes the relationship between your CoG and the opponent, creating new problems.

Turn, Rotation, Axis Shift, Reorientation, Angular Adjustment.

The key is to distribute weight so the axis is stable during the rotation.

Arm Drag, Rolling Back Take, Back-Step.

it's everywhere.
