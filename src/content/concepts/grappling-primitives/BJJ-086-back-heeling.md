retracting your heel backward into your own body to generate clamping pressure.  Hamstrings for knee flexion.  Tension and rotational torque.

armbars; back-heel is a primary control of the torso/shoulder, to get your hips closer to their center of mass.

leglocks; back-heel above their knee to lock it into place.

triangle, body triangle, leg clamps; back-heeling is everywhere.

common mistake; extend or relax the legs too much, this can open space and reduce pressure.
