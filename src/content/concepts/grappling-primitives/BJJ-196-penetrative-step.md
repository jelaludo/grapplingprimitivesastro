From wrestling.  

It's about closing the distance and getting deep into enemy territory, not just reaching with your arms but crowding their space with your body, stepping through them with intent and base.

In judo it might an aspect of Tsukuri for some hip throws, deep step.

It’s not enough to "reach", you must enter with intent, putting your whole structure into the throw or takedown. 

Shallow step leads to half-hearted shot or throw.
Deep penetrative step leads to their CoG being disrupted.
