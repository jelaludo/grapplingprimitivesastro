The "other limb" principle. In most limb attacks (armbar, knee-bar, heel hook), the free limb is a large part of any escape attempts.  90% (made up number) of leg lock defense comes from the other leg. A free hand grabbing your arm-pit can shut down a kimura. 

Think about a standard armbar from guard. You isolate one arm, great. But if their other hand is free to grip, stack, or posture, that armbar is going nowhere. The same logic applies on defense: when someone attacks your right arm, your left hand is your best rescue tool.  Zero-sum.

Your free limb is your best tool to defend the other one being attacked; deny that when you're on the offense.
