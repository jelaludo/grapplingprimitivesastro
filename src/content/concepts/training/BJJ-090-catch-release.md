When there's an obvious experience discrepancy, you get close to lock-in a submission (the “catch”) to the point where you know you could have gotten it (don't lie to yourself either), then let go without finishing (the “release”).

- You to practice control and precision

- They learn and keep rolling without constant resets

- A more flow-based, educational sparring experience

It’s a sign of control, trust, and teaching mindset, not a missed opportunity to assert dominance on a noob.
