Soak up all the information.  Take notes.  Switch your brain on, make connections yourself between the disparate lessons and nuggets of wisdom.  Take ownership of your training.  especially in the early years of training, you should accumulate hundreds of ideas and concepts and techniques, then as "your belt darkens" you start to sieve through and keep what works best for you, refine.
You can't refine something you don't have.
