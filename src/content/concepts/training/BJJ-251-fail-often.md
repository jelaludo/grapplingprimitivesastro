Fail Often, Fail Early.

As long as you learn from your mistakes! it is crucial to let yourself fail often and early to get your of your mat time.  

not every roll has to be a measure of your manhood and battle to the death.

recognize the mistake that got you in a bad spot, tap, file your questions to ask at the end of class.

Fail often so you can multiply your chances of learning.
