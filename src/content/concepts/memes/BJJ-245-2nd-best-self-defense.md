"BJJ is the 2nd best for self defense, the 1st is Concealed Carry" -- Jockko

There's an interesting discussion to be had about "Self-Defense" vs "Most Effective to Knock Out on the concrete"

Boxing, Muay Thai, Letwhei, etc. would probably do a better job than BJJ at quickly knocking down a threat, brain damage and all, in a few seconds.

Grappling requires a bit of set-up and control, but there's the case that it's precisely these elements that help protect the attacker that make it good "self-defense", in a non-life-or-death situation perhaps.

if you're in a warzone, get weapons.
