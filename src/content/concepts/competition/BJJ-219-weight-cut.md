Plan early to cut weight, starts weeks out, don't aim for an unrealistic weight class.

Reduce your intake but don't remove anything entirely.  Don't shock your system too much.

Make sure that you're hydrated on the day of the competition.

Dangers of Bad Weight Cuts:
Dehydration : kidney damage, fainting, impaired cognition, reduced reaction time, reduced endurance

cramping, dizziness, overheating

fasting reducing gut function and lowering immune defense

mental fatigue, irritability, mood swings

muscle loss

making weight at the expense of power and health might not be worth it.

If you don't feel sharp on the day, you did something wrong.

avoid panic cuts.
