title: "Types of Grappling Arts"
---
There's only one human body, default is  four limbs and one head.
There are many variations on how to grab each other to control, submit or inflict pain.

One modern way to categorize might be
- Sport (Rule sets)
- MMA / Vale Tudo (Rule sets + striking awareness)
- Self-Defense (No rules + striking awareness)
- LEO, Military (heigthened stakes up to death)
- Art (intellectual and aesthetic pursuit via the body)

## Ancient Grappling Traditions

### 𓂀 Egyptian Wrestling

![Image](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/0/09/Beni_Hassan_tomb_15_wrestling_detail.jpg)

![Image](https://iiif.wellcomecollection.org/image/M0006216/full/800%2C/0/default.jpg)

![Image](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/09/Beni_Hassan_tomb_15_wrestling_detail.jpg/799px-Beni_Hassan_tomb_15_wrestling_detail.jpg)

![Image](https://www.thearmwrestlingarchives.com/uploads/5/2/3/2/52328353/benihassan1_orig.jpg)

**Date:** ~2000 BCE  
**Region:** Ancient Egypt (Beni Hasan tombs)

- One of the **oldest recorded grappling systems**.
    
- Tomb murals depict **hundreds of techniques**: throws, trips, joint locks.
    
- Suggests a **highly structured wrestling curriculum**.

![Image](https://www.dynastyclothingstore.com/cdn/shop/products/Shuai-Jiao-Chinese-Wrestling-Till-I-Die-Bomber-Jacket-Canvas-Bomber-Jacket-Dynasty-Clothing-MMA.jpg?v=1671239529&width=2000)

![Image](https://cdn.britannica.com/78/12678-004-4E87B773/Cornish-wrestling.jpg)

![Image](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/12/Collar_and_elbow_wrestling_match.jpg/250px-Collar_and_elbow_wrestling_match.jpg)

**Region:** Western Europe (Britain, Brittany)

Examples:

- **Gouren** – Brittany
    
- **Cornish Wrestling** – England
    
- **Scottish Backhold Wrestling**
    

Mostly **upright jacket or belt wrestling styles**.

### Catch Wrestling

![Image](https://fanaticwrestling.com/cdn/shop/articles/unnamed_9_4ba75609-96af-4b77-96ab-23b84ec0ffe3_1024x1024.png?v=1697120017)

**Region:** United Kingdom / USA

- "Catch-as-catch-can"
    
- Known for **submission holds and leg locks**.
    
- Influenced **pro wrestling, sambo, MMA**.

## Other Notable Grappling Styles

Additional grappling traditions worth listing:

- **Glima** — Iceland (~900 CE)
    
- **Kurash** — Central Asia (~1000+ years)
    
- **Bokh** — Mongolian wrestling (~13th century)
    
- **Luta Livre** — Brazil (~1920s)
    
- **Kosen Judo** — Japan (~1914)
    
- **Freestyle Wrestling** — Europe / USA (~1904 codified)
    
- **Greco-Roman Wrestling** — France (~1848 codified)
    
- **Combat Sambo** — USSR (~1938)
    
- **Submission Grappling / No-Gi** — Global (~1990s)
